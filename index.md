---

I'm currently in my second-year pursuing a Ph.D. in computer science at EPFL, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Ola Svensson. I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science, wehre my research focuses on the study of approximation algorithms for problems in the area of combinatorial optimization, and beyond worst-case analysis.
